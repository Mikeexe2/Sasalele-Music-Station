Tips Welcome to Sasalele Music Station!

To enhance your experience, simply click on the name of the radio station to be redirected to its homepage. If the radio is currently playing, click on the radio icon instead. Also, feel free to share your favorite songs in chat session.

Use Shazam extension on your browser to identify current playing song

Radio not playing? If the radio isn't playing on Chromium-based browsers like Chrome, Vivaldi, Opera, Edge, etc., it is due to the default restrictions on mixed security content or CORS. This affects many streams since it is served via HTTP and not HTTPS.

To fix this, click the lock icon in the URL bar, go to 'Site Settings', find 'Insecure content', and allow it. Alternatively, use Firefox as it doesn't block unencrypted pages.
