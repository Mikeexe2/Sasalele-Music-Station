Sasalele Music Station – Google Drive Music Player (GDMP)

This application connects to your Google Drive and allows you to browse, play, and download your personal audio files directly in your browser. All file access happens locally in your session, and no audio data is stored or transmitted outside your device.

Supported features:

  • Playback support for MP3, OGG, FLAC, WAV, AAC, M4A, and WebM audio formats.
  • Reads embedded audio metadata and album artwork.
  • Download audio files directly to your device.
This application only requests read-only access to your Google Drive in order to display and stream your audio files. It cannot modify, upload, or delete your files.

Steps:

  1. Open your folder in Google Drive .
  2. Look at the URL in your browser.
  3. Copy the text that appears after /folders/.
  4. Paste the value into the Folder ID field.

Example URL:

https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1A2B3C4D5E6F

Folder ID in this example:

1A2B3C4D5E6F

You can also enter root to browse your top-level Google Drive folder after signing in.

Steps:

  1. Open your folder in Google Drive.
  2. Right-click the folder and select Share.
  3. Set access to Anyone with the link.
  4. Copy the generated share link.
  5. Paste the link into the Public Drive Link field and click Load Link.

This method lets you browse public folders without signing into Google.
