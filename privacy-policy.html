01 Overview

Sasalele Music Station – Google Drive Music Player ("the App", we", "our", or "us") is a personal music library viewer that connects to your Google Drive account to let you browse and download audio files you own. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with full transparency.

Plain-language summary: We only access your Google Drive to show you your own music files. We do not store your files, sell your data, or access anything beyond what you explicitly authorise.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what controls you have. By using the app, you agree to the practices described here.

02 Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate the service:

  • Google Account information: Your email address and basic profile data provided by Google at sign-in, used solely to identify your session.
  • Google Drive metadata: File names, file IDs, MIME types, and folder structure of your Drive content — retrieved on demand to render the file tree in the app.
  • Audio file access links: Temporary, user-initiated download links for audio files you choose to download. These links are generated by Google and are not stored by us.
  • Session tokens: Short-lived OAuth access tokens, stored temporarily in your browser's local storage for the duration of your session (maximum 1 hour). These are never transmitted to or stored on our servers.

We do not collect, read, or store the content of your audio files or any other files in your Drive.

03 Google OAuth & Permissions

The App uses Google OAuth 2.0 to authenticate you and to access your Google Drive on your behalf. We request the following Google API scopes:

Scope Purpose Classification
https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.readonly Read file names, folder structures, and generate download links for audio files you own in Google Drive. Sensitive
https://www.googleapis.com/auth/userinfo.email Retrieve your email address to maintain your session and enable session refresh. Basic
https://www.googleapis.com/auth/userinfo.profile Retrieve your basic profile name for display purposes within the app. Basic

We only request read-only access. The app cannot create, modify, move, or delete any files in your Google Drive. Access is strictly limited to reading file metadata and providing download links for audio files you explicitly select.

Our use of data obtained through Google APIs complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

You may revoke The App's access to your Google account at any time by visiting Google Account Permissions.

04 How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate you and maintain your session for up to one hour.
  • To retrieve and display your Google Drive folder structure and audio file list within the app interface.
  • To generate download links for audio files you explicitly choose to download.
  • To refresh your OAuth session token when it expires, using the hint email stored locally in your browser.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, analytics, machine learning, or any purpose beyond the direct operation of the service described above.

We do not use your Google user data to develop, improve, or train any generalised AI or ML models.

05 Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Google LLC: Requests are made to Google's APIs to retrieve your Drive data. Google's own Privacy Policy applies to data held by Google.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, and only to the extent legally required.

There are no advertising partners, analytics platforms, or data brokers involved in the operation of the app.

06 Data Storage & Security

The App operates primarily as a client-side application. All Google Drive data is fetched directly in your browser and is never transmitted to or stored on our servers.

  • OAuth access tokens are stored in your browser's localStorage and expire after 1 hour.
  • Your email address hint is stored in localStorage solely to enable seamless session refresh.
  • No audio file content is cached, uploaded, or stored by the app at any point.

While we implement reasonable security practices, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure. You are responsible for ensuring your own device and browser session security.

07 Data Retention

Because we do not operate backend servers that store your data, there is no server-side retention to speak of. All data held by the app exists only in your browser's local storage and is cleared when you:

  • Sign out of The App,
  • Clear your browser storage or cookies, or
  • Revoke access via your Google Account settings.

08 Your Rights & Controls

You have full control over your data and access. Specifically, you may:

  • Revoke access: Remove the app's access to your Google account at any time at myaccount.google.com/permissions.
  • Clear local data: Delete all locally stored session data by clearing your browser's local storage for this site.
  • Request information: Contact us to request a description of any data we hold about you (in practice, this will be none, as we hold no server-side data).
  • Data deletion: Since no personal data is stored server-side, deletion is accomplished by revoking access and clearing browser storage.

If you are located in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or other jurisdictions with applicable data protection laws, you may have additional rights including the right to access, rectify, or port your data. Contact us to exercise these rights.
